P&P by GPT

P&P by GPT uses AI to bring Jane Austen’s “Pride and Prejudice” to life in a new way. The story has been retold by ChatGPT, highlighting its main events and characters. AI-generated illustrations from DALL-E add a visual touch to the narrative, making it more engaging and fun to explore. This project shows how technology can make classic literature more accessible and enjoyable for everyone.
